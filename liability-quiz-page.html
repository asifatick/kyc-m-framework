Know Your Coverage

Liability: The Best Defense…

Fire, earthquake, burglary… these are hazards we can prepare for

But what if a visitor or somebody who works for you is accidently injured in your home or a member of your household damages a neighbor’s property. How will you pay for that? The liability coverage in your homeowners policy is designed to cover these potentially staggering costs. This includes expenses associated with your legal defense and possible court awards. The only certain thing in life is uncertainty. This quiz will help you build and test your knowledge about liability insurance because, well, accidents can happen...
